Our Story

A family tradition of excellence in the heart of Condado

Where Passion Meets Prime

PRIME GRILL 1302 was born from a passion for creating an exceptional dining experience in one of San Juan's most vibrant neighborhoods. Our family-style steakhouse combines the warmth of traditional hospitality with the sophistication of prime, hand-cut meats.

Located in the upscale Condado district, we source the finest ingredients from local Puerto Rican farmers and artisans. Every cut of meat is carefully selected and aged to perfection, ensuring an unforgettable culinary experience.

From our signature steaks to our fresh seafood and creative dishes, each plate tells a story of quality, dedication, and love for the art of fine dining.
